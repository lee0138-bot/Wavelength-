Self-Awareness Hub

Decoding what you feel.

Emotions are biological weather patterns. Identifying your current status helps you manage it safely.

⛈️

Anxiety & Panic

Feels like a racing heartbeat, shallow breathing, circular thoughts, or a knotting sensation inside your stomach.

🌧️

Heavy Sadness

Feels like heavy limbs, low mental or physical battery power, an urge to isolate, or crying without an obvious reason.

⚡

High Stress

Feels like physical tension, irritability over tiny things, jaw clenching, and feeling completely pulled in ten directions at once.

🌋

Hot Anger

Feels like flushing heat, a sudden burst of extreme energy, defensive reactions, or a strong urge to snap or smash things.

🌌

Loneliness

Feels like an empty distance in your chest, thinking that nobody around relates to you, or feeling isolated even inside crowds.

🌊

Burnout & Overwhelm

Feels like an emotional blackout where your brain feels frozen and completely unable to process any more assignments or input.
